About

Relaxio is designed to allow users to create and store their own ambient mixes for reading, relaxing, and focusing. It is inspired by and dedicated to the entire ASMR community.

This app was developed as my capstone project in the Front-end Web Development course at Bloc.io. Significant contributions to the development work were made by Matthew Maxwell, my Bloc mentor. The app logo, sound, and play/pause icons were all designed by Gisele Schaaf.
